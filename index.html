Our Profile

Yorozuya Gin-chan is an all-purpose service company operating in Kabukicho, Edo. We’re ready to take on all kinds of jobs, big or small, with one goal in mind: solving your problems—though we often end up creating new ones along the way.

Our mission is simple: we do anything you pay us for. From everyday chores to the strangest requests, we won’t turn down a job as long as the payment is right (well… sometimes even when it’s not). We are your one-stop solution for all those unexpected needs.

  • Daily Task: Shopping, cleaning, and companionship.
  • Searches: Finding lost items, pets, or people
  • Problem Solving Special missions, fighting bad guys, or handling things no one else can

Contact us, and we guarantee your problem will be solved… somehow.
